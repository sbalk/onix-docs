Heartbeat#

Authors:

Jonathan P. Newman

Version:

1

IO:

Frame Source, Register Access

ONIX ID:

12

ONIX Hubs:

PCIe Host

Description#

The Heartbeat device produces periodic samples containing the hub clock count. It is useful for generating keep-alive signal for various communication channels.
